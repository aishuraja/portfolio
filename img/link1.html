5 KILLER TIPS TO CREATE YOUR BUSINESS WEBSITE MORE IMPACTFUL….

Hello everyone! In our day-to-day life , fortunately or unfortunately we are referring through different kinds of websites . These websites play vital role in everyone’s life.

Websites are most commonly used for business perspective. So this website is a powerful tool to do business among people and the main moto is to seek attention from viewer’s towards the website.

Your website should be created in such a way that the viewers of your website should be converted into customers. So we call this process as “conversion”.

“Everyone can develop a website but the best website develops you”.To seek attention from people towards your website, here I provided some short tips to improve and develop your website.

To make your website more impact just follow these five phases;

Phase 1: ANALYSIS…

Now it’s your time to analyse for what we are creating websites- “ website purpose”.

And the main moto for creating your website- “website goals”

So you have made analysis just only for audience attention- “ Target audience”.

Phase 2: PLANNING…..

Your plan for your website should contain:

  • Sitemap
  • Advanced technologies
  • A good website structure.

Phase 3: DESIGN…

To make your website more attractive you should design your website with,

  • Visual style
  • Wireframe models

Phase 4: CONTENT…

Your website must contain right text- “copywriting”

And some attractive photos and videos should be added.

Your website should have SEO- Search engine optimization.

Phase 5: DEVELOPMENT , TESTING , DEPLOYMENT….

These websites are made only with HTML and CSS codes. And they are developed using database and responsive design.

After developing our website it’s time to test our website. These testing involves :

  • Technical testing
  • Browser compatibility
  • And it’s purpose had been fulfilled.

Atlast here comes deployment…

Website are monitored , bug fixed and they are maintained.

So I hope these tips can make your website more impact.
