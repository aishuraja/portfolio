DEVOURING CARTOONS…

Hello everyone! I am the person who love to explore things dramatically which doesn’t happens in my real life…I used to wonder why I am so dramatic and abnormal about things which usually doesn’t workout in my life..and the reason behind my dramatic way of thinking started from my childhood which exists from cartoon…

Aishu Raja
Apr 25 · 3 min read

I am towards this lousy cartoons. These lousy cartoons gives good and more information in a pictorial format . Recently I read a novel “ This one summer”. This is about two friends “ Rosy” and “ Windy”. The main attractive thing in this novel is about lousy cartoons. The story was quite interesting but these cartoons made the story much more interesting.

Mostly these lousy cartoons are used in comics. However these cartoons are not so good yet it seeks attention towards it. Not only children but also teens like me and adults are so attracted towards these lousy cartoons.

I wish to share an unforgettable incident which was made by this lousy cartoons. Usually I used to go for a trip. And recently I went to Delhi and when I say delhi , everyone thinks about “Taj mahal” , once I visited taj mahal I was astonished by seeing those architecture works. And there I met an artist who is very good in drawing cartoons. He was selling the cartoon picture of Taj Mahal …and that picture was amazing.

By seeing taj mahal I was amazed by their architecture works but by his cartoons i was made and felt complete. His cartoon explained me the history behind their architectural design and construction.

Culture of North Indians are totally different from our culture. I loved their attire . I noticed many things and there way of worship was different and amazing.

So these lousy cartoons created curiosity towards reading novels , comics and so on.

There are various cartoon characters which exists in many cartoon networks yet I love these lousy cartoons which gives me complete feel.

Many social messages and information are mostly conveyed through this lousy cartoons. The main reason of this cartoons are ,it easily seeks attention not only from children but also from adults.

These lousy cartoons were given emotions. They can be expressed according to the situation (happy, sad, lazy,bored…and so on..). These cartoons were given life so they create curiosity and also awareness among people.

Lousy cartoons plays vital role in our day to day life. So I hope without these cartoons our life wouldn’t be so interesting , easy and made colourful❤️.
